# whoami

Hi there. This is me:

My name is Isabel Meraner. I am computational linguist, super-passioned for biodiversity and for the preservation of botanical heritage knowledge.

From natural language processing of biodiversity literature to the maintainance of a fungi database and frontend development for field projects on endangered lichens, I am thrilled to experiment with state-of-the-art technologies to make biodiversity and sustainable agriculture more visible.

I love being who I am, doing what I do, and changing what I think needs to be changed. Currently, I live and work remotely on different projects on a farm in the Tuscan hills.

# teaching

Life is all about learning, it is up to us to see the world through new eyes that are willing to learn.

Besides some teaching experience in German "Deutsch als Fremdsprache" (DaF) at the Goethe institute, Bologna and in a 1:1 student refugees program, I am currently working on a new teaching project, "La Fattoria Didattica". Using the farm as a peaceful collective setting, we are developing an inclusive space for people from allover the world regardless of age, gender and gender identity, race, ethnicity, religion, sexual orientation, disability, economic status and other diverse backgrounds.

Please reach out, if you have a specific course topic in mind.

# biodiversity excursions

Falling in love with our surrounding biodiversity and the manifold marvels of nature is not hard.
Teaching other people to do the same, is hard...


I noticed during some random hiking trips with friends and family how much I love telling people about trees and shrubs, about wild-growing herbs and their application for natural remedies, about identifying flowers and some easy tricks to distinguish one species from another.

Get in touch if you want to know more about the plants that surround you or if you just want to plan a tailored, informative hike with your work colleagues or family.

Trust me, it's going to be fun!

Qualifications

  • Swiss Botanical Certificate ("Bellis": 200 species)
